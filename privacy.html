🔥 Privacy Policy

Last updated: April 25, 2026

What is Calcifer?

Calcifer is a personal AI assistant built for private use by its owner. It integrates with Google services (Gmail and Google Calendar) solely to help the owner manage their personal information.

Data Collection

Calcifer accesses Google account data (email and calendar) only on behalf of the account owner. No data is collected, stored, shared, or sold to any third party.

Data Use

Google account data is used exclusively to:

  • Read and summarize email on the owner's request
  • Read calendar events to provide scheduling assistance

Data Sharing

No data is shared with any third party. All processing occurs locally on the owner's private server.

Contact

This application is for personal use only. For questions, contact the owner directly.
